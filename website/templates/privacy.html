Privacy

Last Modified: August 7, 2022

Our team believes that privacy is extremely important. This page aims to offer transparency and inform you about the information that this website collects. Furthermore, the underlying code for this website is entirely open source. You are free at any time to visit our github repository to better understand the inner-workings of this site.

Emails and Passwords

We use emails and passwords to generate your account and allow you to login. Accounts are necessary for each applicant to individually track their application cycle(s). We will never disclose your email or password to a third party. All passwords on this site are encrypted.

Application Cycle Information

As a CycleTrack user, you are able to enter information into our website. At a basic level, we collect the years you are applying, schools applied to, and dates of application actions such as submission, interview received and acceptance dates. Additionally, we allow you enter contextual information about your application. This includes GPA, MCAT score, and basic demographic information. This information is anonymously aggregated and may be displayed publicly on our website or in other publications to offer insight into the medical school admissions process. To avoid the possibility of user identification, we do not display information about schools with less than 5 applicants. Furthermore, you may enter information about courses taken in school to use the GPA calculator tool. All information is entirely voluntary for you to enter.

CycleTrack Profiles

CycleTrack profiles allows you to publish a custom summary of your application cycle(s). Creating a profile is voluntary and information displayed on a profile is selected by the profile author. Profiles are private (only accessible to the author by default) and must be published by the author to become publicly accessible. The CycleTrack team does not monitor the content of profiles, however, inappropriate content is subject to removal and may be reported by emailing admin@cycletrack.org.

Cookies and Google Analytics

This website uses "cookie" messages to allow you to login and logout of the site.

Our website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service offered by Google that tracks and reports traffic to our website. Google uses the data collected to track and monitor the use of our Service. This data is shared with other Google services. Google may use the collected data to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. You can opt-out of having made your activity on CycleTrack available to Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. The add-on prevents the Google Analytics JavaScript (ga.js, analytics.js and dc.js) from sharing information with Google Analytics about visits activity. For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms web page: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Modifications

We maintain the option to amend this privacy policy at any time, so please review it periodically. Your continued use of this website following the date of a posted amendment to our privacy policy will be considered your agreement to the changed terms.
