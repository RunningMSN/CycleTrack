Privacy

January 30, 2022

Our team believes that privacy is extremely important. This page aims to offer transparency and inform you about the information that this website collects. Furthermore, the underlying code for this website is entirely open source. You are free at any time to visit our github repository to better understand the inner-workings of this site.

Emails and Passwords

We use emails and passwords to generate your account and allow you to login. Accounts are necessary for each applicant to individually track their application cycle(s). We will never disclose your email or password to a third party. All passwords on this site are encrypted.

Application Cycle Information

As a CycleTrack user, you are able to enter information into our website. At a basic level, we collect the years you are applying, schools applied to, and dates of application actions such as submission, interview received and acceptance dates. Additionally, we allow you to enter information about your application. This includes GPA, MCAT score, and basic demographic information. We use this information to add context to application cycle actions. This information is anonymously aggregated and summaries may be displayed publicly on our website to offer insight into the medical school admissions process. To avoid the possibility of user identification, we do not display information about schools with less than 5 applicants. All application cycle information is entirely optional for you to enter.

Cookies

This website uses "cookie" messages to allow you to login and logout of the site.

Modifications

We maintain the option to amend this privacy policy at any time, so please review it periodically. Your continued use of this website following the date of a posted amendment to our privacy policy will be considered your agreement to the changed terms.
