CycleTrack is your application companion

We are a free platform designed to help you keep track of your medical school applications, visualize your data, and make the application process more accessible and transparent for all applicants.

Track Your Applications

For any cycle you apply, generate your school list or import one from a spreadsheet. Throughout the cycle, keep track of your applications by entering dates of actions. Your data will anonymously join that of other applicants in the school explorer where you can see a real-time summary of application actions or school statistics.

    Secondary Essay Optimization
    About This Tool

    Submitting secondary applications in a timely manner is hard. This tool uses historic data to find the latest date by which a completed application was included in the next interview invitation pool. It is unique to your school list.

    By combining your individual school list with historic data, this tool is meant to provide a suggested order in which to complete applications. It will automatically update as you track data in the school list.

    The following is a recommended order in which to submit your secondary essays based on historic data. Bolded lines are applications which are mandatory to submit based on the deadline you have entered. Unbolded dates are not hard requirements, and are purely suggested based on the last application submission in the previous cycle that yielded the next interview if it were to occur in the current year. Dates in the past are provided with a 4 day margin of error before recalculating.

    *Note: We recommend completing all applications as soon as possible. This order is a data-informed suggestion for how to organize your time.

    # School Date
    1 A.T. Still - Arizona (DO) ~Jul 16
    2 Chicago (MD) ~Jul 17
    3 University of California Los Angeles (MD) Jul 22
    4 Alabama (MD) ~Aug 04

    We do not have enough data to provide suggestions for: Baylor (MD-PhD)

    As this is a new tool, it may still have some bugs. If something doesn't look right, please submit a bug report.

    CycleTrack does not guarantee the accuracy of this tool. The information presented is purely a suggestion based on users' self-reported data from the previous cycle.
