CycleTrack is your application companion

We are a free platform designed to help you keep track of your med school applications, visualize your data, and make the application process more transparent and accessible.

Application Tracking

For any cycle you apply, generate your school list or import one from a spreadsheet. Throughout the cycle, keep track of your applications by entering dates of actions such as submission of secondary applications or interviews. Your data will anonymously join that of other applicants in the school explorer where you can see a real-time summary of application actions or school statistics.

    Crowdsourcing Data

    Data entered into CycleTrack is used to make applications more transparent and more accessible. The school explorer presents aggregated de-identified data about the progress and statistics of individual medical schools. Additionally, data may be used for research about the medical school application process. We always de-identify all applicant information to maintain privacy.

    Other Tools

    We currently offer a GPA calculator for calculating cumulative/science/non-science GPA using criteria specified by AMCAS, AACOMAS and TMDSAS.
